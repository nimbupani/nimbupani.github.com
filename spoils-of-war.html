Spoils of War

Outlook has been one magazine that has been anti -US and anti-war in most of its web articles -but not before raising some very relevant questions. I am not sure if what U.S is doing can be considered appropriate (it is surely not morally right!). I guess morality has no chance in today’s world -except as an ace used to shoot down the evidently “morally weak”.
