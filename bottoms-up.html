Bottoms Up!

What do you say about a prof who encourages you to drink by buying you free beer, gin, red and white wine? Well, nothing except “Cheers!” :) My FMI tutor ( read about him here), held the last tutorial in the NTU Staff Club (which is the pub in NTU). Here, he treated all of us to a gala free drinks session. Of course, most of the students were goodie goodie guys and girls ( yeah, me too!) - which explained our hesitation in gulping down glass after glass of alcoholic drinks.

But yeah! a good ending to a glorious semester of Financial Management and Institutions!

P.S: Yeah, I am still intoxicated ;)
